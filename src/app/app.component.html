Add your STAR
and make the sky
a little brighter.

Last year the community joined together to send gratitude to the many doctors, nurses, and staff at South Brooklyn Health during difficult times. We showed our thanks. Now, we’re growing it with the Sky of STARS.

Send your thanks to caregivers by adding a STAR to the night sky. As messages are sent in, the sky grows brighter day by day. The brightening sky symbolizes exceptional care to our patients. Nurture the sky’s growth and become part of this living installation. Let’s give South Brooklyn Health staff a sky bright with gratitude.
